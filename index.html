Spooky Halloween Apple Smiles

Description

This spooky halloween treat is certainly tough to beat. With just a few simple ingredients, you can make a treat your kids will love and be excited about for your next halloween! You can even make it a fun activity for the family! Feel free to substitute anything and make it as nutritiously appropiate as needed.

Ingredients

  • Whole Apples (Honey Crisp and/or Granny Smith preferred)
  • A Bag of Small Marshmallows
  • Peanut Butter (Smooth or Chunky)
  • Optional: Chocolate or Strawberry Syrup

Directions

  1. Cut off the stem and root ends of the apple. This will help keep unnecessary foliage out of your treat.
  2. Cut the apple into 5 vertical segments around half an inch thick. This should leave you with 5 saucer-shaped apple slices.
  3. Cut the apple saucers in half, to create "lips" for the smile of your spooky apple smiles.
  4. Cover one side of each apple slices with your peanut butter of choice. Dont be afriad to be generous if you or your kid love peanut butter!
  5. Place 4 to 5 marshmallows on top of the peanut butter covered side of the apple slice to create a "teeth" effect.
  6. Finish off by stacking another apple slice on top, with the peanut butter covered side facing the uncovered marshmellows.

Voila! Now you have Spooky Halloween Apple Smiles!

As a bonus, you may dip the spooky smiles in your preferred syrup to give them a "bloody" effect for added spookiness!
